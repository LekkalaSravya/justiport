About Us

Learn About Us

Justiport is a commercial litigation firm with offices in California and the Pacific Northwest. The firm’s attorneys provide prompt expert legal advice on complex legal matters at the pre-litigation, litigation, trial and appellate levels for national financial, insurance and real estate institutions.

Our attorneys often advise and work directly with the executive offices and their general counsel’s offices handling matters that have a national reach. The firm’s attorneys have represented clients in hundreds of mediations, arbitrations and trials in state and federal courts and venues throughout the country.
